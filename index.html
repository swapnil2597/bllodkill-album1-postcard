Bloodkill was formed in late 2016 with Vishwas and Yash being the founding members. The band started off as a typical thrash metal outfit influenced by the 80’s era of Metallica, Slayer, Megadeth, Kreator, Exodus, etc. However, with a few lineup changes the dimensions of Bloodkill’s music broadened as they added elements of sub genres like Heavy and Groove metal.

Genre: Heavy/Thrash Metal

Location: Mumbai, India

Release Date: Unreleased

Album Credits: Produced by Keshav Dhar (Skyharbor) at Illusion studio. Album artwork and Layout by Acid toad.

Throne of Control

  Bloodkill

The band released their first single ‘3B’ in 2017, followed by ‘Horrorscope’ in 2018. Bloodkill is set to release their debut album titled ‘Throne Of Control’.

The album contains an eclectic range of songs to offer, from the Old school thrash metal goodness of the Title-track and ‘Visions’, the mid-tempo thrash with a good dose of old school heavy metal in tracks ‘False Face’ and ‘Unite and Conquer’, to the melodic and groovy ‘For I am the Messiah’. The record also consists of reimagined rendition of ‘3B’ with the new lineup along with a remixed and remastered version of ‘Horrorscope’.
